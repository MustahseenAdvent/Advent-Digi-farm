Our Services

Services

Category

Customized Image Dataset

Drone-based aerial imagery database establishment through automated waypoint and mapping algorithm. Datasets are updated for every season once/multiple times depending on specific needs, to help acquire better crops through predictive analytics.

Category

Satellite and Weather Dataset

Satellite data and images are updated and obtained every 2-4 days. Historical archive satellite data is available too. Statistical vegetation indices such as Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI), Enhanced Vegetation Index (EVI), National Resource Inventory (NRI), Disease Stress Water Index (DSWI), Normalized Difference Water Index (NDWI) is given for all possible vegetation areas. Accumulated temperature, precipitation, soil temperature, and moisture on different depth are extracted from satellite data. Historical weather archive and forecast are provided.

Services

Category

Data Analytics Dashboard

Dashboard is a visual service that helps monitor your field states over the year. The service allows to operate with satellite imagery and weather data along with advanced machine learning technologies.

Category

AI and ML based Predictive Algorithm

AI/ML-based predictive algorithm reports include overviews of the conditions of the observed crops, distribution on the map of the type of crops and their condition, vegetation statistics, etc. Data can be stratified and aggregated by any criterion, for instance, by crops, by regions, by years. Based on large amounts of processing satellite and climate data, we provide analytical reports and detailed datasets for crop monitoring, Recognising field boundaries, Vegetation indices statistics by each recognized field and Climate data for regions and particular fields.
